## 🚨 **CRITICAL PITFALLS TO AVOID**

### **AI/UX Pitfalls**
- ❌ Avoid sending large prompts over unstable connections
- ❌ Never block UI while AI loads
- ❌ Ensure AI requests are idempotent and retry-safe
- ❌ Don't show long-winded responses on small screens

### **Mobile Development Pitfalls**
- ❌ Avoid asking for permissions up front — wait until relevant
- ❌ Don't store entries only locally — sync needed
- ❌ Use lazy loading for historical data
- ❌ Implement proper offline-first strategy

### **AI Integration Pitfalls**
- ❌ Add backoff logic if OpenAI is down or rate-limited
- ❌ Token guardrails: summarize or trim before long prompts
- ❌ Monitor AI response quality and user satisfaction
- ❌ Implement proper fallback systems
